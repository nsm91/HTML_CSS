Latitude vs Humidity

The purpose of this project was to prove a well understood concept, that average temperature rises as one approaches the equator.
To do this, we pulled real time weather data on ~500 cities through the OpenWeatherMap API, imported the results into Pandas, then used Matplotlib to visual the results.

There doesn't seem to be that strong of a realtionship between latitude and humidity. It looks that at the equator the humidity is always a little higher, but that's about it. Perhaps one could say that the humidity in the southern hemisphere looks a little lower, which could be explained by the lower seasonal air temperature.
