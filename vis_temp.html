Latitude vs Temperature

The purpose of this project was to prove a well understood concept, that average temperature rises as one approaches the equator.
To do this, we pulled real time weather data on ~500 cities through the OpenWeatherMap API, imported the results into Pandas, then used Matplotlib to visual the results.

As was expected, clearly the temperature did increase as one approached the equator. What as perhaps the most interesting discovery was that it was slightly skewed toward 20 degrees north. This has to do with the tilt of the earth - it was summer in the northern hemisphere at the time. It was interesting to me to witness such a large swing.
